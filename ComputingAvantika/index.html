How to make Pasta Salad

Pasta salad is a versatile and refreshing dish that combines al dente pasta with a medley of fresh vegetables, tossed in a flavorful dressing. Its vibrant colors and diverse textures make it a delightful addition to any summer gathering or as a satisfying standalone meal.

ingredients

  • 225g of pasta of your choice (e.g., fusilli, bow, or penne)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 small cucumber, diced
  • 1 bell pepper (any color), diced
  • 1/4 cup olives, sliced
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, cubed
  • 1/4 cup fresh herbs (basil, parsley, or cilantro), chopped
  • 1/8 cup Italian dressing or vinaigrette
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

preparation

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente.
  3. Drain the pasta and rinse it under cold water to stop the cooking process.
  4. Wash and chop the cherry tomatoes, cucumber, bell pepper and herbs.
  5. Slice the olives.
  6. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked and cooled pasta with the chopped vegetables.
  7. Toss in the cubed feta and the chopped fresh herbs.
  8. In a separate bowl, whisk together the Italian dressing or vinaigrette, olive oil, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper.
  9. Pour the dressing over the pasta and vegetables.
  10. Gently toss everything together until well coated.
  11. Cover the pasta salad and refrigerate for at least 1-2 hours to allow the flavors to meld.
  12. Before serving, give it a final toss and adjust the seasoning if needed.
